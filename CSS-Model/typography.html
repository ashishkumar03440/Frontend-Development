The Art of Good Typography

Typography is not just about choosing fonts — it’s about creating a visual rhythm that guides the reader’s eyes. A well-chosen font family and balanced spacing can make a world of difference. Designers often experiment with contrast in size, weight, and style to create hierarchy and harmony in their layouts.

In modern web design, using readable fonts such as sans-serif families improves legibility on screens. Proper line-height ensures comfortable reading, while subtle letter-spacing enhances clarity. Typography transforms plain text into a meaningful and enjoyable reading experience.

The goal is simple: create a visual flow that feels natural and balanced. Whether you're designing a blog, magazine, or digital interface, remember that good typography speaks louder than decoration.
